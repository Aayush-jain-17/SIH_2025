About Rooftop Harvesting

Unlike groundwater, rainwater contains very low levels of dissolved salts and minerals, making it ideal for washing clothes, industrial cooling, and even battery water without additional treatment. When excess harvested rainwater is directed underground through recharge wells, it can travel long distances through natural underground channels, improving groundwater levels in surrounding areas, even kilometers away. Less demand on groundwater pumping means lower electricity usage, since pumping deep aquifers requires significant energy. This indirectly cuts down carbon emissions.
